Checkout & Payments

Controls the cart/checkout on the live site. Until your Square account and a payment endpoint are connected, checkout collects the customer's details and places the order as “pay at pickup.” Add the Square IDs below when ready to enable card payment.

Square Payment (fill when ready)

Card data is collected by Square's secure hosted fields — your site never sees raw card numbers. The backend URL receives a payment token (not the card) and charges it via Square's API. Leave blank to keep “pay at pickup” mode.

Calls & Texts

Controls call forwarding and order/pickup texts. These settings publish to the live backend on Deploy. Secrets (Twilio Auth Token / Account SID) are never stored here — they live encrypted in Vercel. This panel only holds non-secret operational settings: phone numbers and message text.

📞 Call Forwarding

Incoming restaurant calls ring this phone. Format: +1 then 10 digits, no spaces or dashes.

"Require key press" lets you decline an unknown call to voicemail without it hitting your personal voicemail.

💬 Order & Pickup Texts

Comma-separated, E.164. Each gets a text with the full order when a customer checks out. Customers always get their own order-number text regardless of this.

Appears in the confirmation text, e.g. "Ready for pickup in about 10-12 min."

🔗 Twilio Webhook URLs

These tell Twilio which URL to call when your number gets a voice call or text. Pointing them at your Vercel endpoints below means calls/texts route through this site's serverless functions instead of you setting it manually in the Twilio Console. Apply pushes these straight to your Twilio number using the account credentials already in Vercel — no console login needed.

🎛️ Twilio Control

Full messaging & voice control without opening the Twilio Console. Locked behind your admin passcode (the ADMIN_API_TOKEN you set in Vercel) — it's saved in this browser only, never published to the site.

🔒 Secrets — set in Vercel, not here

TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID, TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN, TWILIO_CALLER_ID (your Twilio business number) live in Vercel → Settings → Environment Variables. If you change your Twilio number or rotate the token, update it there and redeploy. Everything on this page is safe to edit and publish.
