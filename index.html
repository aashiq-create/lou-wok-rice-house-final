Our Story

Lou Wok Rice House was built for the transplants, the foodies, the people who moved to Phoenix and couldn't find the flavors they grew up with.

We built Lou Wok to fill that gap — a fast, bold, unforgettable rice house that brings Midwest comfort food energy to the Valley of the Sun. Every plate wok-fired to order. Every time.

FAQ
How far in advance should I book?▾
We recommend 3–4 weeks for smaller events and 6–8 weeks for large events or weekends.
Do you travel outside Phoenix?▾
Yes — full Valley coverage including Scottsdale, Tempe, Mesa, Chandler, and Gilbert. Travel fees may apply beyond 20 miles.
What's the minimum guest count?▾
Minimum 20 guests for food truck bookings. Drop-off catering has a $150 minimum order value.
Can I customize the menu?▾
Absolutely. Select the Custom Package and tell us what you need. We accommodate dietary restrictions and can create off-menu specials.
What is your cancellation policy?▾
Cancellations 14+ days out receive a full deposit refund. Within 14 days, the deposit is forfeited. We'll always try to reschedule.
