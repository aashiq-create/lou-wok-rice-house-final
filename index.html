Home Base
North STL to Phoenix

Lou Wok Rice House is rooted in the Chinese restaurant culture of St. Louis, Missouri — the rice houses of North County, the corner spots on Lindbergh, the places where your family ordered by the bucket and the St. Paul sandwich came wrapped in wax paper.

Lou Wok is for the transplants who couldn't find it here. For the St. Louis expat who still dreams about the rice house on the corner. Every plate is made to order. The way it was always supposed to be.

See the Full Menu
The STL Rice House Tradition

St. Louis has a unique Chinese-American food culture unlike anywhere else — egg foo young, wok fried rice by the half and whole, crab rangoon with every order, and the St. Paul sandwich that no other city has. Lou Wok carries that tradition west.
