Our Story

Lou Wok Rice House was built for the transplants, the foodies, the people who moved to Phoenix and couldn't find the flavors they grew up with.

We built Lou Wok to fill that gap — a fast, bold, unforgettable rice house that brings Midwest comfort food energy to the Valley of the Sun. Every plate wok-fired to order. Every time.
